The problem.

When nesting a floated div inside another div, the default behavior is for the child to extend past the boundary of its container. This behavior is not a bug. It is correct behavior according to the W3C. Furthermore, this behavior is generally consistent across all browsers.

However, although the behavior may be correct, it is usually not desired. There are multiple approaches for "fixing" this problem.

In my case, I don't care about IE6 compatibility and the clearfix solution appears to be superior. See examples below.
